What is Agentic AI?

Understanding the next evolution of artificial intelligence

Beyond Traditional GenAI

Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can act independently to complete tasks in the real world, not just respond with information like GenAI. These AI agents are designed with reasoning abilities, can plan actions, remember past interactions, interface with applications, learn over time, and most importantly, operate fully autonomously unless instructed otherwise.

Key Capabilities

Autonomous Reasoning

Makes decisions and plans actions independently based on context and goals

Memory & Learning

Remembers past interactions and continuously learns from experience

System Integration

Interfaces with applications, APIs, and tools to execute real-world tasks

Action Planning

Develops and executes multi-step plans to achieve complex objectives
